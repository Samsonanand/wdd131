About Vandaloor Zoo

Vandaloor Zoo, officially known as Arignar Anna Zoological Park, is one of India's largest and most diverse wildlife parks. Established in 1855, the zoo houses over 2,500 animals across 171+ species, providing an enriching experience for visitors of all ages.

Explore the vast landscapes, observe majestic wildlife, and engage in conservation efforts while enjoying a memorable adventure!
